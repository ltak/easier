# Workout System

## Context

Training matters because body goals are not just about calories. Performance, recovery, and adherence shape the outcome.

## Problem

Overbuilt workout systems create noise. Underbuilt ones miss the connection between intake, recovery, and progress.

## Solution

Start with structured, repeatable plans and simple progression rules that connect back to nutrition signals.

## Output

### V1 scope

- full-body and upper-lower plan templates
- structured workout logging
- simple progression decisions based on reps, load, and completion
- recovery signals informed by calorie intake and protein consistency

### Acceptance criteria

- Users can follow a structured plan and log completed sets.
- The system can recommend a next-step increase, hold, or regression deterministically.
- Nutrition signals can influence recovery messaging without overriding progression rules.
